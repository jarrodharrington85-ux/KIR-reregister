Get your re-registration plan

Kiribati is moving to the new Business Names Act. All business name owners must re-register on the Online Business Registry. There is no fee, and MTCIC no longer accepts paper forms.

Re-registration has four steps:

  1. 1Create your online account
  2. 2Link your online account to your business
  3. 3Confirm your business name details
  4. 4Submit your form and required documents through your online account

It takes place over several days, with short waits while MTCIC reviews each step.

After re-registration: annual returns

You must file an annual return every year to keep your business name. MTCIC emails you when it is due; if it is not filed, your name will be removed. Learn about annual returns.
