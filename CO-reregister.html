Kiribati is moving to the new Companies Act 2021. All companies must re-register on the Online Business Registry. There is no fee, and MTCIC no longer accepts paper forms.

Re-registration has four steps:

  1. 1Create your online account
  2. 2Link your online account to your company
  3. 3Confirm your company details
  4. 4Submit your form and required documents through your online account

It takes place over several days, with short waits while MTCIC reviews each step.
